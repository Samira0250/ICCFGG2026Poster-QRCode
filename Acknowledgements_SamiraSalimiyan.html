Acknowledgements

The authors would like to thank the staff at the Scott-Ritchey Research Center, specifically Jessica Cannon and Dr. Stephanie Maples. Funding was provided in part through support from the Scott-Ritchey Research Center, the Animal Health and Disease Research Program, and a grant from the Auburn University Research Support Program. Special thanks to the Department of Pathobiology and the Computational Biology Program at Auburn University for providing research resources and inspiration.

Figures were created with BioRender.com. Data analysis and visualization were performed using R (v4.5.1) with packages including ggplot2, tidyverse, and qrcode. Statistical analyses were conducted using GraphPad Prism 10. Cell-type deconvolution was performed using RnaXtract, CIBERSORTx (Stanford University), and HIDE (Hierarchical cell-type Deconvolution). Human scRNA-seq reference data were obtained from the Allen Brain Atlas. Feline snRNA-seq reference data were derived from the publicly available dataset described in the bioRxiv preprint: “Single Cell Multiomics Across Nine Mammals Reveals Cell Type Specific Regulatory Conservation in the Brain” (Anderson et al., 2025; https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.08.06.668931 ).
